## There's a Better Way to Understand What's Happening

Most explanations fall into two traps:

**The Magical Story:**  
"It's basically conscious."  
Feels intuitive. Wrong.

**The Reductive Story:**  
"It's just autocomplete."  
Technically true. Explains nothing useful.

This guide offers a **third path** — one that actually helps you predict, understand, and work with these systems without the confusion.

First, think of an LLM as something you **navigate**, not something you talk to.

The map is not the territory, and your expectations redraw the map as you walk.
